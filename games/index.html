What We Do

In the Game Design Club, we create our own games, all the way from the initial concept to a physical or digital version for people to play! These can be card games, online games, board games, and any other medium we find interesting.

NVWK

Some would say that this game is not very well known...

NVWK is the first ever game developed by the Game Design Club way back in 2017, it was continually updated and improved until 2021. It's an elaborate game of strategy, but if you play your cards right you might survive.

TAPP is an intricate card game where two games will never be the same. Fight your custom character against your opponents in multiple combat phases using unique and powerful weapons to eliminate their health!

Desert Pioneers

The desert tells a different story every time someone ventures into it...

Desert Pioneers is a quick game of strategy and luck. You are a mercenary sent by your ruler to a desert. To claim the land for your ruler, you must complete your unique task before the others.
